What is

SEO

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. As name indicates it is a skill which is required to optimize your website based some factors.

What It is so important?

As we all know there are thousands of websites, articles are there in the internet and thousands of blogs been uploading on daily basis. Tracking them, indexing them is not an easy job. There the SEO concept comes into picture. SEO helps to rank your website on the search engine.

Some of the best SEO practices you can follow in order to rank higher

  • Don't use high quality images.High quality images eat up lot of space in servers along with that they take higher time duration for loading.
  • Use relevent keywords in headings and description
  • Do some keyword research before writiing any article you can do that in SEMRush or Keyword tool.
  • Write a article which has 500-2000 words. Remember the thumb rule longer the qulity article more possiblility of getting rank
  • Write articles by your own don't copy it from other creators google has ways to identify the copied articles.
